Eat breakfast, lunch, or dinner at the most special restaurants in the heart of the city. Here are our tips!

Pancake Bakery

Treat your tastebuds to a Dutch culinary classic at the Pancake Bakery. It's the most famous pancake restaurant in the Netherlands and has had guests like Leonardo DiCaprio and Paris Hilton. They have pancakes for anyone's taste. Try the authentic Dutch pancake with stroop (syrup) or get one of the 25 international pancakes. They are open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, making it a perfect spot for any meal of the day.

Visit their website for more information

Silveren Spiegel

Located in a historic building from the 17th century, Silveren Spiegel is an upscale restaurant known for its exquisite Dutch and French cuisine. The ambiance is cozy and elegant, perfect for a romantic dinner or a special celebration. Enjoy a seasonal menu prepared with fresh local ingredients, and be sure to try their famous poached pear dessert!

Get more details on their website

Rainarai

Experience a unique fusion of Thai and Dutch cuisine at Rainarai. This restaurant is known for its vibrant atmosphere and delicious dishes that combine traditional Thai flavors with local ingredients. Whether you're in the mood for spicy curries or fresh seafood, Rainarai has something for everyone. Don't miss their special weekend brunch!

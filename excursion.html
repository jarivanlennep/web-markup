THIS IS HOLLAND

Experience an absolutely spectacular SD flight simulator that makes you feel like you're flying over the best parts of the Netherlands. This is part of their hour-long program where they also teach you about Dutch history and interesting information about the Netherlands such as the fact that we live under sea level and how we battled with that. So, whether you're a thrill seeker or a history lover or you like to find out what makes the Netherlands special, make sure to come to this hour-long adventure.

LOVERS Boat Tour

In this time of the year the evenings are becoming darker quicker and Amsterdam starts to get beautiful light artworks all around the city. This boat tour takes you on a journey where you fall in love with Amsterdam's night view and watch the Amsterdam Light Festival. Instead of having to walk for a whole day to see all the artworks, you can just relax and sit for an hour in a boat and see everything. This is also a good date idea as the views are very romantic.

Get more information and buy tickets here

Yellow Bike Tour

Explore Amsterdam like a biking local and see the most beautiful spots. Bike through the canals and streets with a tour giving interesting historical information, so you know more about what you're looking at. You'll come across Rembrandt's house, the Magere Brug, and many more iconic spots in Amsterdam. The tour is two hours long, and it doesn't matter if you're a beginner or advanced biker.

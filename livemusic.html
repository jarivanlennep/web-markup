Maloe Melo

Maloe Melo is the oldest blues and rock cafe in Amsterdam. It's a small, intimate place where the locals go. A lot of famous blues and rock musicians have played here. They have live music seven days a week from 9 PM until 3 AM. If you want to see where the locals go for live blues and rock, check it out!

The full agenda is on their main website

Alto

This is another intimate live music spot for jazz lovers. It has a cozy and artistic vibe with lots of photos of famous musicians. It's also close to Museumplein, so after checking out the Van Gogh Museum or Rijksmuseum, you can give it a visit. If you're looking for some serious live jazz performances, Alto is a must.

Further information and opening hours on their website

The Melkweg

If you're not looking for a small cozy place with jazz or blues and just want to go out, the Melkweg is the place to go. Translating to "Milky Way" in Dutch, the Melkweg used to be a milk factory. Now the large interior space is used as a venue for music every night. Whether you're a hip-hop fan, an indie fan, or a pop fan, Melkweg has you covered.
